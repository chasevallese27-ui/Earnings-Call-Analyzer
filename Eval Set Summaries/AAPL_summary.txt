Company: AAPL

# Apple Q2 FY2025 Earnings Call Analysis

## 1. KEY FINANCIAL METRICS

**Revenue:**
- Q2 FY2025: $95.4 billion (up 5% YoY from $90.8 billion)

**EPS:**
- Q2 FY2025: $1.53 (up 8% YoY)

**Gross Margin:**
- Q2 FY2025: 46.6% (flat YoY from 46.6%)

## 2. FORWARD GUIDANCE

For Q3 FY2025, Apple provided the following guidance:
- Revenue: $85-88 billion range
- Gross margin: 45.5%-46.5%
- Operating expenses: $14.3-14.5 billion
- Tax rate: approximately 16%
- Other income/expense: approximately $300 million

Management noted the midpoint implies modest growth from the prior year period, with some caution around macroeconomic and tariff environment impacts on supply chain and demand in certain geographies.

## 3. MANAGEMENT SENTIMENT

**Mixed to Cautiously Optimistic**

Management displayed confidence in long-term fundamentals while acknowledging near-term challenges. Positive sentiment was evident regarding Services growth, Apple Intelligence engagement, and global market records. However, cautious tone emerged around China market competition from Huawei, macroeconomic uncertainty, and tariff dynamics creating supply chain concerns.

## 4. KEY RISKS

• Competition from Huawei in China's premium smartphone segment
• Macroeconomic uncertainty affecting global demand
• Tariff dynamics creating supply chain and cost pressures
• Currency headwinds impacting gross margins
• Demand softness in Greater China market
• Product cost dynamics pressuring margins

## 5. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Apple delivered solid Q2 results with 5% revenue growth to $95.4 billion and 8% EPS growth to $1.53, driven by record Services revenue of $24.2 billion and strong performance across most product categories. The company faces headwinds from intensifying competition in China, particularly from Huawei's premium segment recovery, alongside broader macroeconomic and tariff uncertainties. Despite near-term challenges, Apple remains confident in its long-term position with growing installed base, strong Apple Intelligence engagement, and continued Services momentum.
