# MINISO Group Q4 2025 Earnings Call Analysis

## 1. KEY FINANCIAL METRICS

**Revenue:**
- Q4 2025: RMB 6.25 billion (32.7% YoY growth)
- Full Year 2025: RMB 21.44 billion (26.2% YoY growth)

**EPS:**
- Q4 2025 Adjusted Diluted EPS: 9.4% YoY growth
- Full Year 2025 Adjusted Diluted EPS: 7.8% YoY growth

**Gross Margin:**
- Q4 2025: 46.4% (vs 47% in Q4 2024)
- Full Year 2025: 45% (flat vs 2024)

## 2. FORWARD GUIDANCE

Management provided the following outlook for 2026:
- Group revenue growth expected at high teens rate
- 3-year revenue CAGR from 2023-2026 to be no less than 22%
- Same-store sales in key markets (China and North America) to maintain healthy low single-digit growth
- New store additions planned at 510-550 stores for full year
- Both adjusted operating profit and adjusted net profit expected to accelerate growth rate in 2026
- Q1 2026 revenue growth expected to be no less than 25%
- China same-store sales to maintain high single-digit growth in Q1 2026
- North America same-store sales to deliver strong mid- to high double-digit growth in Q1 2026

## 3. MANAGEMENT SENTIMENT

**Bullish** - Management expressed strong confidence and optimism across multiple fronts. The CEO emphasized record-breaking performance milestones, successful strategic initiatives like MINISO Land stores, and robust growth in proprietary IP (particularly UU brand achieving RMB 165 million in Q1 2026). Management highlighted their transition from investment phase to profitable growth phase in the U.S. market and expressed confidence in Mexico market potential. The tone was consistently positive about strategic execution and future growth prospects.

## 4. KEY RISKS

• Margin compression from direct operated store expansion, particularly in early-stage international markets
• Inventory management challenges in overseas markets, with international inventory turnover at 228 days vs domestic 74 days
• Southeast Asia market performance challenges requiring significant operational adjustments
• Tariff headwinds and macroeconomic uncertainties in key markets
• Competitive pressure in domestic China market requiring continuous same-store sales improvement
• Execution risk in accelerating store renovation program (targeting 80% of stores for upgrade)
• Dependence on IP strategy success for maintaining growth momentum

## 5. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

MINISO delivered exceptional Q4 2025 results with revenue reaching RMB 6.25 billion (32.7% YoY growth) and full-year revenue of RMB 21.44 billion, driven by strong performance across all business segments including 112% growth in TOP TOY and successful MINISO Land store rollout. The company has transitioned to a higher-quality growth model with fewer new store openings but stronger same-store sales growth, particularly achieving mid-teens domestic same-store growth in Q4 and over 20% in the U.S. market. Management expressed strong confidence in their multi-brand, multi-IP strategy, particularly highlighting the success of proprietary IP UU which generated RMB 165 million in Q1 2026 alone, while providing bullish guidance for 2026 with high-teens revenue growth and accelerating profit growth expected.
